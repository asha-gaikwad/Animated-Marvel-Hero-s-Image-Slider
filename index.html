IRON MAN
Iron Man, a Marvel Comics superhero, is the alter ego of billionaire genius Tony Stark. After creating a high-tech suit to save his life, he uses it to combat evil and protect humanity. Stark is a key member of the Avengers, known for his intelligence, charisma, and heroic transformation.

CAPTAIN AMERICA
Captain America, a Marvel Comics superhero, is the alter ego of Steve Rogers. Enhanced by the Super-Soldier Serum, he possesses superhuman strength, agility, and endurance. Known for his unyielding courage, leadership, and iconic shield, he is a key member of the Avengers.

THOR
Thor, a Marvel Comics superhero, is the Norse God of Thunder. Wielding the enchanted hammer Mjolnir, he possesses immense strength, weather manipulation, and flight. As a key member of the Avengers, Thor is known for his bravery, godly heritage, and commitment to protecting both Asgard and Earth.

HULK
The Hulk, a Marvel Comics superhero, is the alter ego of Dr. Bruce Banner. When angered, Banner transforms into the Hulk, possessing immense strength and invulnerability. Known for his unparalleled power and rage, the Hulk is a key member of the Avengers.

DOCTOR STRANGE
Doctor Strange, a Marvel Comics superhero, is a master of the mystic arts and the Sorcerer Supreme. His powers include spellcasting, dimensional travel, and manipulation of time. Known for his complex character and magical prowess, he protects the Earth from supernatural threats.

SPIDER MAN
Spider-Man, a Marvel Comics superhero, is the alter ego of teenager Peter Parker. Equipped with spider-like agility, web-slinging abilities, and wall-crawling skills, he uses his powers to protect New York City. Known for his resilience and sense of responsibility, Spider-Man is a beloved figure in the superhero community.

BLACK PANTHER
Black Panther, also known as T'Challa, is the king of the advanced African nation of Wakanda. His suit, made from the rare metal vibranium, grants him superhuman strength, agility, and durability. As a superhero, he combines royal leadership with exceptional combat skills and a commitment to protecting his people.

X-MAN
X-Men, created by Marvel Comics, are a team of superheroes with unique mutant abilities. Key members include Wolverine, Cyclops, and Jean Grey, each possessing extraordinary powers. The team fights for a world where mutants and humans coexist peacefully.
